SOILESS CULTIVATION FOR RAPID BIOMASS FEEDSTOCK PRODUCTION

Achieving climate change targets requires rapid solutions. Our innovative soilless cultivation approach does just that. Biomass? Afforestation? Multi-purpose crops?

Rapidly grow superior energy crops, using novel aeroponic technologies, to support UK Net Zero

In order to meet our near-term climate change targets, we need to plant large amounts of trees and bioenergy crops. This takes time and could lead to negative impacts from land use change. Our technology can solve this problem for biomass growers and the bioenergy industry using innovative soil-free technology. 

Who will benefit from our research?

Independent of soil quality, our approach results in 25% faster growth and 100 % increase in above-ground biomass. Pre-empting potential climate change challenges of water scarcity and land usage, the closed-loop system requires limited amounts of water and fertilisers and limits any leaks of chemicals into the environment. It complements traditional practices of biomass production and offers a rapid solution to the fast deployment of biomass.
